What We Do

Expertise where it matters most

Four pillars of financial leadership, tailored to your stage and ambition.

Reporting & Insights

Board-ready management reports, KPI dashboards, and data-driven insights that translate numbers into decisions. Know exactly where you stand — and where you're heading.

  • Monthly management accounts
  • KPI & dashboard design
  • Investor reporting packs
  • Variance analysis & commentary

Budgeting & Forecasting

Robust financial models and rolling forecasts that give you a clear runway. Plan with confidence, allocate resources wisely, and stay ahead of cash flow surprises.

  • Annual budget creation
  • Rolling cash flow forecasts
  • Scenario & sensitivity modelling
  • Headcount & resource planning

Financial Control & Redesign

Tighten processes, reduce leakage, and build a finance function that scales. From chart of accounts to approval workflows — we redesign your financial operating system.

  • Process & controls audit
  • Chart of accounts restructure
  • Accounts payable/receivable optimisation
  • Systems selection & implementation

Debt & Capital Raising Advisory

Whether you're raising equity, securing debt facilities, or preparing for exit — we build the financial narrative and models that get deals done.

  • Investor deck & financial model
  • Debt structuring & lender negotiation
  • Due diligence preparation
  • Term sheet review & advisory
